Welcome to the Tutorial


To navigate through the site, use the navigation bar at the top of the screen to move around.


Click on Login at the top right of the screen or on the far right of the navigation bar to sign in to your account. If you are a new user, you can register as an instructor or student with one of the two buttons at the bottom of the sign in page.


Click on the Course button from the navigation bar to see all available courses offered by the school.


To file a complaint against a fellow student or any faculty member, click on the complaints button found at the navigation bar.

Student View

This is the student account page. It is what the students see when they click on account in the navigation bar. In the personal record is where you will see all your classes and the grade along with your current GPA. In the apply for Graduation, you will be sent to graduation if it works or you will be sent to a warning screen.


As a student, you will be able to register for courses during the course registration period. To access this page, click on course registration under the Manage Courses button on the navigation bar. To add a class into cart, click on More Details on the right of the class you want then add to your shopping cart. Once you are done shopping, click on the register button underneath Currently in Cart: to register for next semesters classes. You will be able to see what classes you are taking next semester under the Next Semester Classes: table.

Instructor View

This is the teachers account page. Instructors will be able to see more than the students after clicking on account in the navigation bar.
